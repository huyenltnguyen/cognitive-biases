The first half of this module covered how groups shape decisions — through conformity pressure and through groupthink. In both cases, the bias operates on what the group decides.

The next section covers a different effect: how being part of a group changes the way we judge individual people. The same behavior, performed by two different people, can be interpreted in opposite ways depending on how we relate to the person doing it.
