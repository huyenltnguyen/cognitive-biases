Knowing the name of a bias doesn't protect you from it. Evidence suggests that awareness alone rarely changes behavior. What changes is your ability to recognize the feeling: a decision that came together very quickly; a piece of evidence you find yourself dismissing; a strong pull to continue something that isn't working.

The next part builds that recognition skill, one pattern at a time.
